Research Interests

My research focuses on advancing natural language processing technologies and their applications in information systems. I'm particularly interested in embedding models, domain adaptation of large language models, and financial NLP applications.

Financial NLP

Developing specialized NLP models for financial text analysis, sentiment detection, and market prediction.

LLM Adaptation

Techniques for efficiently adapting large language models to specialized domains with limited data.

Embedding Models

Dedicated to designing and optimizing text embedding models for professional domains like finance.

Business Applications

Practical implementations of NLP in business intelligence, decision support, and customer service.
