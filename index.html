About My Research

My research focuses on advancing natural language processing technologies and their applications in information systems. I'm particularly interested in:

  • Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) for knowledge-intensive tasks
  • Domain adaptation of large language models
  • Financial NLP applications
  • Instruction fine-tuning and in-context learning
  • Efficient model architectures for embedding generation

I aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge NLP research and practical applications in business and finance contexts.

Current Focus: Investigating how to improve the efficiency and reliability of LLM-based embedding models. I am also exploring the potential of finance-adapted LLMs.
